# Location Prompts (場景提示詞庫)

**使用說明：**
在生成分鏡或最終畫面時，請將這些場景 base prompts 與「人物 prompt」及「動作描述」結合。所有提示詞預設加上 `--ar 16:9 --style raw --v 6.0` (以 Midjourney 為例)。

**全片一致原則：**
- 香港段落以冷調、雨水反光、擁擠空間、低飽和復古城市質感為主，不走 cyberpunk 霓虹方向。
- 台灣段落以暖調、陽光、開闊空間、自由感與生活氣息為主，而且**不是回憶濾鏡**。
- 全部場景都應保留 cinematic atmosphere、analog texture、真實材質與空氣感。

## 1. 香港：忘憂舊書店 (Wang You Bookstore, HK)
**視覺核心：** 壓抑、安全感、冷暖對比 (窗外冷藍雨景 vs 房內暖黃鎢絲燈)、擁擠的木質空間。

- **Wide (建立鏡頭 / 全景):**
  `wide establishing shot, interior of a cramped vintage bookstore in Hong Kong, floor-to-ceiling dark wooden bookshelves overflowing with old books, dim warm tungsten lighting contrasting with cold cyan daylight from a rainy window, melancholic and quiet atmosphere, cinematic lighting, analog texture, highly detailed`
- **Medium (對話鏡頭 / 中景背景):**
  `medium shot background, inside a vintage bookstore, a cluttered wooden counter, stacks of old books, dust motes floating in soft warm light, a rainy window with cool blue tones in the blurred background, shallow depth of field, cinematic, natural texture`
- **Detail (特寫 / B-roll 空鏡頭):**
  `extreme close up, dusty old books on a dark wooden shelf, a vintage porcelain teacup, soft warm desk lamp light, heavy rain droplets on the window glass in the out-of-focus background, rich textures, cinematic, film grain`

## 2. 香港：雨季街道 (Rainy Streets, HK)
**視覺核心：** 潮濕、冷調 (青藍色 / 灰綠色)、狹窄、壓抑、招牌與雨水倒影。

- **Wide (建立鏡頭 / 全景):**
  `wide establishing shot, a narrow Hong Kong street during a heavy rainstorm, wet asphalt reflecting muted vintage shop signs and streetlights, sloped road, oppressive and melancholic cinematic atmosphere, cyan and cool blue color grading, realistic rain texture`
- **Medium (跟拍 / 中景背景):**
  `medium shot background, a rainy Hong Kong street under a vintage canvas awning, heavy rain pouring down, blurred umbrellas and old signage in the background, cool blue and gray-green palette, shallow depth of field, cinematic`
- **Detail (特寫 / B-roll 空鏡頭):**
  `close up, heavy rain hitting a puddle on a dark asphalt street, blurred reflections of warm streetlights and faded shop signs in the rippling water, moody cyan tones, cinematic texture, high contrast`

## 3. 台灣：花蓮海岸公路 (Coastal Highway, Hualien, TW)
**視覺核心：** 釋放、生命力、暖調 (金黃色 / 海藍色)、廣闊、強烈陽光。

- **Wide (建立鏡頭 / 全景):**
  `wide establishing shot, a beautiful coastal highway in Hualien Taiwan, bright sunny day, sparkling deep blue ocean, golden sunlight, lens flare, wide open space, sense of freedom and youth, vibrant but natural colors, cinematic wide angle`
- **Medium (騎車 / 對話 / 中景背景):**
  `medium shot background, on a sunny coastal road, sparkling blue sea and clear sky in the blurred background, strong warm sunlight, vibrant and energetic atmosphere, open airy feeling, cinematic`
- **Detail (特寫 / B-roll 空鏡頭):**
  `close up, a vintage film camera resting on a motorcycle seat, bright warm sunlight hitting the lens, ocean waves blurred in the background, golden tones, nostalgic youth, highly detailed, analog texture`

## 4. 台灣：九份 / 夜市 (Night Market / Jiufen, TW)
**視覺核心：** 煙火氣、溫暖、橘紅色調、燈籠、擁擠但充滿活力。

- **Wide (建立鏡頭 / 全景):**
  `wide establishing shot, a bustling Taiwan night market street in Jiufen, glowing red lanterns hanging above, steam rising from food stalls, warm orange and golden lighting, vibrant and lively atmosphere, cinematic, lived-in textures`
- **Medium (人群穿梭 / 中景背景):**
  `medium shot background, inside a crowded vibrant night market, glowing warm lanterns, steam and smoke, blurred crowds in the background, warm golden and orange tones, shallow depth of field, cinematic`
- **Detail (特寫 / B-roll 空鏡頭):**
  `close up, thick steam rising from a night market food stall, glowing warm lantern light illuminating the smoke, vibrant orange tones, rich textures, cinematic lighting`
